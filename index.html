About Bollyfit

Discover the magic of Bollywood dance in the heart of Antwerp

Welcome to Bollyfit Antwerp

Bollyfit Antwerp brings the vibrant energy of Bollywood dance to Belgium. Founded by Nikita Mittal, our studio offers professional dance instruction that combines traditional Indian dance forms with contemporary fitness techniques.

Whether you're a complete beginner or an experienced dancer, our classes are designed to help you improve your skills, boost your confidence, and have fun while getting a great workout.

Meet Your Instructor

Learn from the best in Bollywood dance

Nikita Mittal

Founder & Lead Instructor

With over 10 years of professional dance experience, Nikita brings passion, expertise, and energy to every class. Trained in classical Indian dance forms as well as contemporary styles, she creates a unique fusion that makes Bollyfit classes both authentic and accessible.

Nikita's teaching philosophy centers on creating a welcoming environment where students of all levels can explore the joy of movement while building confidence and community.
